How to Play

Flash Party is an action-packed, fast-paced brawler that blends strategy, quick reflexes, and exciting combat. Here’s a simple guide on how to get started.

  • 01

    Choose Your Character

    When you first start, you’ll be introduced to a diverse roster of heroes, each with unique abilities, playstyles, and stats. From powerful brawlers to speedy, tactical fighters, there’s a character to suit every type of player. Choose your favorite, or try a few to find the one that matches your playstyle!

  • 02

    Understanding the Controls

    The controls in Flash Party are designed for simplicity and precision. Whether you're on a mobile device or playing on a PC, you'll use basic movement and attack buttons to engage in combat.

  • 03

    Matchmaking and Modes

    Flash Party offers various modes to enjoy, whether you want a quick match or a more competitive challenge.

  • 04

    The Goal: Knock Your Opponents Out

    The primary goal in Flash Party is simple—knock your opponents out of the arena! You’ll need to deplete their health by landing hits, dodging their attacks, and using your character’s special abilities strategically. Each time you hit an opponent, you’ll deal damage. If an opponent’s health gets too low, it’s easier to knock them off the stage. Keep an eye on your own health too, and avoid getting hit by opponents who are targeting you!

  • 05

    Learn Each Character’s Abilities

    Each hero in Flash Party has a unique set of abilities. Some characters specialize in close combat, while others can attack from a distance or control the battlefield with traps or environmental manipulation. Take time to learn how your character’s skills work and practice executing them in the heat of battle.

  • 06

    Mastering the Arena

    Each battle takes place in a dynamic arena that may include hazards or obstacles that you can use to your advantage. Be mindful of the layout—use platforms to gain higher ground, avoid dangerous areas, and use the environment to knock opponents off the stage. Each arena has different features, so understanding them will give you a strategic edge.

  • 07

    Collecting Power-ups

    During your match, you’ll find various power-ups scattered around the arena. These items give you temporary boosts, such as increased damage, speed, or even healing. Be quick to grab them, as they can turn the tide of battle in your favor.

  • 08

    Keep Practicing

    The more you play, the better you'll get. Try out different characters, experiment with new strategies, and practice your timing. As you level up and win more matches, you’ll unlock new characters, skins, and other rewards.

    Always be aware of your surroundings and keep moving to avoid being an easy target.

    Push your enemies into dangerous areas, or use platforms to gain an upper hand.

    In team modes, communication and teamwork are key. Coordinate with your teammates to trap and eliminate opponents.

    If you’re new to the game, start by battling AI opponents to learn the basics before jumping into multiplayer matches.
